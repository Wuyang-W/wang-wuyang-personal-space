WANG Wuyang

A Space of Photography, Gastronomy and Poetry

我没有寻找诗，诗也没有寻找我。我们只是偶遇。

WANG Wuyang (or WANG Zhelun, Andrew WANG) is a Chinese student learning in France. He was born in Shandong, China in 1997 and he started his journey in Europe in early 2016.

He started to write poems when he was 15. Later, when he came to France, he was obsessed with the world of Gastronomy. When he moved to Paris for his Master's degree, he met several friends - artists and photographers - and he started to take photos ever since.
